Instructions For Installation

  • Install the plugin
  • Open new tab and visit to Amazon.com
  • Click on "Allow" notifications. (Chrome will not show any notifications if you don't allow notifications)


  • Click on your Cart


  • Click on "Checkout Amazon Fresh Cart" or "Checkout Whole Foods Market Cart" according to your preferences


  • Click "Continue" until you land "Schedule your order" page. Leave the tab open. You will get a notification when the delivery window is available.
